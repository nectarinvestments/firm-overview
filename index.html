The Opportunity
How we deploy in this environment

Three structural advantages let Nectar access deals that no other provider can efficiently fund.

AI-native platform
AI agents run every part of the investment process — origination, underwriting, monitoring. This lets us fund deals that are too small for institutions (sub-$5M) and too complex for banks (pref / mezz / equity structures).
Existing network
We have multi-year partnerships with sponsors who weathered the downturn. Now we partner with them to take advantage of the market correction. They provide boots on the ground, we provide the capital.
Outsized returns
Institutions finance larger deals, earning 10–12%. We earn a 2–6% premium by tackling smaller, more complex opportunities that they cannot efficiently originate or manage.
Use cases

Acquisition equity gap

Sponsors acquiring distressed assets at 20–40% below 2022 pricing need equity to close. We fill the gap between the senior loan and their contribution.

Debt buydown / bridge to refi

Bridge debt from 2021–22 is maturing above current appraised values. Lenders require partial paydowns. We provide the capital.

Sponsor platform equity

Proven operators scaling acquisition programs now that pricing has reset. We provide sponsor-level capital across a pipeline of deals.

CapEx Financing

Senior lenders now require a capital injection before extending. We find opportunities with strong fundamentals and provide capital to improve the property and increase occupancy.
